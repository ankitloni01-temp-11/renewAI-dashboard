SURAKSHA TERM SHIELD - POLICY DOCUMENT v1.0
IRDAI Registration No: 107 | UIN: SLISLA21047V012122

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Suraksha Term Shield is a pure protection term life insurance plan. It provides a high sum assured at an affordable premium. This is a non-participating, non-linked, pure risk life insurance plan.

2. ELIGIBILITY
- Entry Age: 18 to 65 years
- Policy Term: 10 to 40 years
- Premium Payment Term: Regular pay (same as policy term), Limited pay (5/7/10 years), Single pay
- Minimum Sum Assured: Rs. 25,00,000
- Maximum Sum Assured: No limit (subject to underwriting)

3. PREMIUM DETAILS
- Annual Premium Range: Rs. 12,000 to Rs. 48,000 (standard rates)
- Premium depends on age, sum assured, term, and smoking status
- GST applicable at 18% on term plans (standard rate)

4. DEATH BENEFIT
On death of the life assured during the policy term:
- 100% of Sum Assured paid to nominee as lump sum
- No survival benefit on maturity (pure term plan)
- Death benefit is tax-free under Section 10(10D)

5. GRACE PERIOD
- 30 days grace period for annual, half-yearly, quarterly premium payment modes
- 15 days grace period for monthly premium payment mode
- Policy remains in-force during grace period

6. LAPSE AND REVIVAL
- Policy lapses if premium not paid within grace period
- Revival possible within 5 years from date of first unpaid premium
- Revival requires: payment of all overdue premiums + simple interest at 8% p.a. + revival declaration of good health
- Medical examination may be required for revival

7. EXCLUSIONS
- Suicide within 12 months of policy inception or revival: Return of 80% of premiums paid
- No exclusion for death by accident (after first year)

8. TAX BENEFITS
- Premium paid eligible for deduction under Section 80C (up to Rs. 1.5 lakh p.a.)
- Death benefit exempt under Section 10(10D)

9. FREE LOOK PERIOD
- 30 days from receipt of policy document (15 days for policies purchased offline)
- If cancelled within free look period: Full premium refunded minus stamp duty and medical charges

10. NOMINATION
- Policyholder must nominate a beneficiary
- Minor nominees require an appointee
- Nomination can be changed at any time during policy term

11. OPTIONAL RIDERS
- Accidental Death Benefit Rider: Additional sum assured on accidental death
- Critical Illness Rider: Lump sum on diagnosis of 34 critical illnesses
- Premium Waiver on Disability Rider: Waives future premiums on total permanent disability
