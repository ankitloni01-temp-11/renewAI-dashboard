SURAKSHA WEALTH BUILDER ULIP - POLICY DOCUMENT v1.0
IRDAI Registration No: 107 | UIN: SLISLA21049V012122

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Suraksha Wealth Builder ULIP is a unit-linked insurance plan that combines market-linked investment with life insurance protection. Premium is invested in chosen funds after deducting charges.

2. ELIGIBILITY
- Entry Age: 18 to 65 years
- Policy Term: 10 to 30 years (minimum 5-year lock-in period)
- Premium Payment Term: Regular, Limited (5/10 years), Single
- Minimum Annual Premium: Rs. 50,000
- Maximum Annual Premium: Rs. 5,00,000 (higher with underwriting)

3. FUND OPTIONS
Available funds:
- Equity Growth Fund: High risk, equity-oriented (expected return 12-15% p.a., not guaranteed)
- Balanced Advantage Fund: Medium risk, 60% equity + 40% debt
- Debt Secure Fund: Low risk, fixed income instruments
- Money Market Fund: Very low risk, liquid instruments
- Index Fund: Tracks Nifty 50 index

4. CHARGES
- Premium Allocation Charge: 0% (waived)
- Policy Administration Charge: Rs. 150/month
- Fund Management Charge: 1.35% p.a. for equity funds; 1.00% for debt funds
- Mortality Charge: Based on age, sum assured, and fund value
- Surrender Charge: Applicable only within 5-year lock-in period

5. DEATH BENEFIT
Higher of:
- Sum Assured (minimum 10x annual premium), OR
- Fund Value at date of death

6. MATURITY BENEFIT
- Fund Value at maturity date (market-linked, not guaranteed)
- Partially or fully withdraw at maturity or continue the plan

7. PARTIAL WITHDRAWAL
- After 5-year lock-in period: Up to 20% of Fund Value per year
- No charges on partial withdrawals after lock-in

8. FUND SWITCH
- 12 free switches per policy year
- Minimum switch amount: Rs. 5,000

9. NAV CALCULATION
- NAV declared daily for all funds
- NAV changes based on market performance
- Current NAV performance: Equity Growth Fund +14% YTD (FY2025-26, not guaranteed)

10. TAX BENEFITS
- Premium eligible for Section 80C deduction (up to Rs. 1.5 lakh)
- Maturity benefit: Tax-free under Section 10(10D) if sum assured >= 10x annual premium
- Note: Budget 2023 - ULIPs with premium > Rs. 5 lakh: Gains taxable as capital gains

11. LOCK-IN PERIOD
- IRDAI mandatory 5-year lock-in period
- Surrenders within 5 years: Surrender value payable after 5 years
- Discontinuation Charge: Rs. 6,000 (Year 1), Rs. 5,000 (Year 2), Rs. 4,000 (Year 3), Rs. 2,000 (Year 4), Nil (Year 5+)
