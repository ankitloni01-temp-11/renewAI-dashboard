SURAKSHA ENDOWMENT PLUS - POLICY DOCUMENT v1.0
IRDAI Registration No: 107 | UIN: SLISLA21048V012122

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Suraksha Endowment Plus is a participating endowment plan that combines life protection with long-term savings. Policyholders participate in company profits through bonuses.

2. ELIGIBILITY
- Entry Age: 8 to 55 years
- Policy Term: 15 to 30 years
- Minimum Premium: Rs. 15,000 p.a.
- Maximum Premium: Rs. 36,000 p.a. (standard plans; higher available with underwriting)
- Sum Assured: 20-30x annual premium

3. MATURITY BENEFIT
- Guaranteed Maturity Sum Assured + Vested Reversionary Bonuses + Terminal Bonus (if any)
- Example: Rs. 18,000 premium for 20-year term = Rs. 5,40,000 projected maturity value
- Bonus rate: Rs. 42 per Rs. 1,000 Sum Assured per year (current rate, not guaranteed)

4. DEATH BENEFIT
Higher of:
- Sum Assured on death + Vested Bonuses + Terminal Bonus (if any), OR
- 105% of premiums paid

5. GRACE PERIOD
- 30 days grace period for all premium modes except monthly
- 15 days for monthly mode
- Policy in-force during grace period

6. SURRENDER VALUE
- Guaranteed Surrender Value: Available after paying 3 full years of premiums
- Special Surrender Value: Higher of Guaranteed or Special Surrender Value payable
- Year 3: 30% of premiums paid, Year 5: 50%, Year 10: 70%, Year 15+: 90%

7. POLICY LOAN
- Available after 3 years: Up to 90% of Surrender Value
- Interest rate: 8% p.a. compounded annually
- Loan to be repaid within policy term

8. REVIVAL
- Revival period: 5 years from date of first unpaid premium
- Overdue premiums + interest at 8% p.a. + revival health declaration

9. TAX BENEFITS
- Section 80C: Premium deduction up to Rs. 1.5 lakh
- Section 10(10D): Maturity and death benefits tax-free (subject to conditions)

10. BONUS HISTORY (Illustrative)
- FY2023-24: Rs. 42 per Rs. 1,000 SA (reversionary bonus)
- FY2022-23: Rs. 40 per Rs. 1,000 SA
- FY2021-22: Rs. 38 per Rs. 1,000 SA
