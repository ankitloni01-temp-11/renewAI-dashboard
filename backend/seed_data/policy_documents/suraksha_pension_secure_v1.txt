SURAKSHA PENSION SECURE - POLICY DOCUMENT v1.0

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Suraksha Pension Secure is a deferred annuity plan that builds a retirement corpus during the accumulation phase and provides regular pension income from the vesting date.

2. KEY FEATURES
- Entry Age: 30 to 65 years
- Vesting Age: 45 to 70 years
- Premium: Rs. 24,000 to Rs. 60,000 p.a.
- Death Benefit: Fund Value or 105% of premiums paid, whichever is higher
- Bonus: Guaranteed Additions of 3% of Annual Premium for first 5 years

3. RETIREMENT OPTIONS
At vesting:
- Commutation: Up to 1/3rd of corpus tax-free as lump sum
- Annuity: Balance must be used to purchase annuity
- Annuity Options: Immediate annuity for life / with return of purchase price / joint life

4. GRACE PERIOD
- 30 days for annual premium payments
- Policy remains active during grace period

5. TAX BENEFITS
- Section 80CCC: Premium deduction up to Rs. 1.5 lakh
- Section 10(10A): Commutation up to 1/3rd tax-free
