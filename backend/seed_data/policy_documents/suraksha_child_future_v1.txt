SURAKSHA CHILD FUTURE PLAN - POLICY DOCUMENT v1.0

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Suraksha Child Future Plan is an endowment plan designed to fund a child's education milestones. On parent's death, premium waiver rider ensures the plan continues without premium payment.

2. KEY FEATURES  
- Entry Age (Parent): 21 to 50 years
- Child's Age: 0 to 12 years
- Maturity: When child turns 18 (typical)
- Premium: Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 30,000 p.a.

3. BENEFITS
- On Maturity: Sum Assured + Vested Bonuses paid when child turns 18
- On Parent's Death: Premium Waiver Benefit (PWB) - future premiums waived, plan continues
- Child receives full maturity benefit at age 18 regardless

4. MILESTONE PAYOUTS
- 25% of SA at child's age 18
- 25% of SA at child's age 20
- 50% of SA + Bonuses at child's age 22

5. GRACE PERIOD
- 30 days grace period for annual/half-yearly/quarterly modes

6. TAX BENEFITS
- Section 80C: Premium deduction
- Section 10(10D): Maturity benefit tax-free
